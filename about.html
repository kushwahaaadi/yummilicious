Our Mission

Yummylicious was created by students, for students. We understand the precious time lost waiting in long lines at campus food outlets. Every minute spent waiting is a minute taken away from studying, assignments, or much-needed rest.

Our mission is simple: Reduce waiting time for university students at campus food outlets so they can use that valuable time for studying instead.

How It Works

1. Browse our selection of campus food outlets and their menus

2. Place your order in advance from anywhere on campus

3. Receive a pickup time notification

4. Walk in, grab your ready order, and get back to what matters
